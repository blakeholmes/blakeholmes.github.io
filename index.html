Fund Investments

Fund investments are diversified by vintage year, stage, industry and strategy. Since its inception in 2002, Sagamore has made over 50 fund investments in China and Global Biotechnology across early-to-late stage funds. The firm focuses on funds with a stable and strong team and proven track record. Over the last six years, Sagamore has established strong relationships with many top managers. The firm also seeks to identify emerging managers who have the potential to be tomorrow’s winners. Sagamore seeks to play an active role and add value post-investment.

Direct Investments

Sagamore seeks direct investment opportunities in non-strategic, but essential sectors in China such as agriculture, financial services and consumer products.

CHINA

Sagamore China Partners (“SCP”) makes private equity fund investments and direct investments in China. SCP I was launched in 2006 and made 14 fund investments through 2008. SCP I is now at the harvesting stage. SCP II was launched in 2010 and made 17 fund investments from 2010 to 2012. The SCP funds can invest up to 20% in direct investments.

BIOTECHNOLOGY

Sagamore BioVentures ("SBV") has made investments in the U.S., Chinese and Singaporean biotech and healthcare markets since 2002, pursuing a strategy involving fund and direct investments. Through four funds, the firm has invested in start-up and growth companies in therapeutics, medical devices, diagnostics and infectious diseases. Since its inception, SBV has made 19 fund investments and 15 direct investments globally.

About

Sagamore Investments was founded in 2002 by Peter Brooks and Rodrigo Yang to make private equity fund and direct investments in global biotechnology and healthcare services. In 2006, the firm commenced a broad investment program targeting growth opportunities in China and in 2010 launched Sagamore China Partners II (SCP II) to make private equity fund and direct investments in China. The firm has staff in Beijing, San Francisco and Hong Kong.
